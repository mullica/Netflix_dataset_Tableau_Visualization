Netflix Dataset observations:

1) In total more than 99 Billion movies and 2 Billion TV Shows were released
from the year 2008-2020.

2) Most Tv Shows or Movies were released in The United States of America with
India being the second. 

3) Top 5 Genres are: 
	> Documentries
	> Stand-up comedy
	> Drama, international movies
	> Drama, independent movies, international movies
	> Comedies, Drama, international movies

4) Most of the Movies and TV Shows were released in the year 2019.

5) More Movies have been released over the years than TV Shows
